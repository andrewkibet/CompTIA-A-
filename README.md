# CompTIA-A-
Summary of CompTIA A+ Certification

As we continue through this course, you may notice that there are many terms we will need to learn. Understanding these terms is an important aspect of the course to complete the certification exam. Be sure to review the learning activities and flashcards in each lesson.

This lesson covered how safety is always the most important concern when working with electronic components and devices, and how the right tools will make the job of repair much easier.

We learned the steps involved in disassembling and reassembling a desktop computer, as well as the steps involved in disassembling and reassembling a laptop computer. Learners evaluated how proper maintenance of desktops, laptops, and all-in-one computers will extend their life and ensure proper operation.
